About Us

Cyber 88 Internet Cafe began offering its service in March 2008 with its first Location on 8815 Roosevelt Ave. 2nd Fl. Jackson Heights NY 11372, in the month of November of the same year due to the influx of customers at that time the second store located on 95-27 Roosevelt Ave, moved later to the 95-02 of Roosevelt Ave, at that time we found many Internet Cafes in the area. On December 2017, we were the two locals that survived in the area, because most people use their cell phones and no longer a computer. Our stores are being set up to offer services each day, and keep helping our customers, letter writing, printing, scanning, computer repairs, telephone payments and Wi-Fi service. We are trying to be hand in hand with the change of technology. We want to continue to provide our best care and provide everything you need for your work, study or daily life.
